Who We Are

Jacuruku

Jacuruku is the land of our birth, once beholden to the glorious empire of Kallor. Long since swept away by the fury of K'rul, and Draconus. With Kallor's empire long gone, the land of Jacuruku has grown fertile, with rich soil perfect for nourishment of the skin. Thus we at Deep Mudding bring various muds from around the world for one to enjoy at our Warren.
